# OrbMech-project
 Interplanetary mission assignment:
The PoliMi Space Agency is carrying out a feasibility study for a potential Interplanetary Explorer Mission visiting three planets in the Solar System: Jupiter, Earth and Mercury. The focus of the study is to analyse all the possible transfer options from Jupiter to Mercury, using a powered gravity assist around the Earth, and determining the optimal departure, fly-by and arrival dates to minimize the cost of the mission (Î”ğ‘£).
